Building a **Web-based School Management System (SMS)** with medium complexity involves a comprehensive suite of interconnected features that manage administrative, academic, and financial operations. 

Core & Advanced Features

The following list of features, compiled from the provided documentation and general industry standards, would be ideal for a medium-complexity system:

Administration & Management

- **Student Information System (SIS):** A centralized database for comprehensive student records, including demographics, health history, emergency contacts, academic history, and disciplinary notes (p. 1).
- **Staff & HR Management:** Modules to manage teacher profiles, leave requests, workload distribution, and basic payroll functionality (p. 1).
- **Online Admissions & Enrollment:** Digital forms that allow parents/students to apply, upload documents, and track their application status, automating the initial workflow (p. 1).
- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):** Customized and secure access dashboards for different user types (administrators, teachers, parents, students), ensuring data confidentiality (p. 1).
- **Automated Timetable Management:** Algorithms that generate conflict-free class and exam schedules based on resource and teacher availability (p. 1).
- **Inventory & Asset Management:** Tracking of school equipment, laboratory supplies, and general infrastructure (p. 2). 

Academic & Learning

- **Learning Management System (LMS) Integration:** A platform for teachers to host digital course materials, assign homework, quizzes, and integrate with external tools like Google Classroom or Zoom (p. 1).
- **Smart Attendance Tracking:** Real-time monitoring via web interfaces or mobile apps, with options for advanced integration methods like QR codes or biometrics (p. 1).
- **Gradebook & Report Cards:** Tools for digital marks entry, weighted average calculations, and the instant generation of customizable digital report cards and transcripts (p. 1).
- **Homework & Assignment Management:** A dedicated system for teachers to post assignments and for students to submit them online. 

Financial & Operational Tools

- **Fee Management & Online Payments:** Automated invoicing and receipt generation with integrated payment gateways for parents to pay tuition via various digital methods (p. 1).
- **Library Management:** A digital catalog to track book inventory, borrow/return history, and automated due date reminders (p. 1).
- **Transport Tracking:** Management of vehicle routes and driver records, which can include real-time GPS tracking for safety and efficiency (p. 1).

- **School Calendar & Event Management:** A shared digital calendar for holidays, exam dates, and parent-teacher meetings (p. 2).
- **Integrated Messaging:** Secure internal messaging systems between teachers, parents, and administration. 

Analytics & Security

- **Reporting & Analytics:** Tools that provide data-driven insights through live dashboards and custom reports on student performance, attendance trends, and financial health.
- **Data Security & Compliance:** Robust security measures, including encryption, cloud storage with backups, and adherence to data protection regulations.
